## Calendar System `[CANON — UESP]`

- Tamriel uses **Eras** as the major unit of time. A new Era begins when a sufficiently world-changing event reshapes political or metaphysical reality.
- Dates are written `<Era abbreviation> <year>`, e.g. `2E 572`, `3E 427`.
- **Era abbreviations:** ME = Merethic Era · 1E = First Era · 2E = Second Era · 3E = Third Era · 4E = Fourth Era.
- The in-game calendar has **12 months of 30 days** plus 3 intercalary days (New Life Festival, Mid Year Celebration, Harvest's End), totalling ~365 days per year. `[CANON — UESP]`

## Dawn Era `[CANON — UESP]`

> **Source:** UESP Lore:Dawn Era — https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dawn_Era  
> **Note:** Events in this era are deliberately non-linear and mythic; different in-universe accounts contradict each other. This is intentional design. `[CONTESTED by design]`

- Exists before linear time. Events cannot be placed on a conventional timeline.
- The **et'Ada** (original spirits, ancestors of gods and Daedra) shape creation from the raw stuff of Aetherius and Oblivion.
- Some et'Ada (the **Aedra**) sacrifice part of their divine nature to create **Mundus** (the mortal plane). Others (the **Daedra**) refuse and retain full power in Oblivion.
- **Convention at Ada-Mantia (the Adamantine Tower):** The Aedra convene and establish the rules governing Mundus. After this meeting the gods depart or go dormant. This event ends the Dawn Era. `[CANON — UESP]`
- The **Heart of Lorkhan** is cast away by the other et'Ada as punishment for tricking them into creating Mundus; it falls to Nirn and becomes the Stone of the Red Tower. `[CANON — UESP]`

## Merethic Era (ME) `[CANON — UESP]`

> **Source:** UESP Lore:Merethic Era — https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Merethic_Era  
> **Note:** Dates in the Merethic Era count *down* (ME 2500 is earlier than ME 1).

| Date | Event | Status |
|---|---|---|
| ME ~2500 | **Ada-Mantia (Adamantine/Direnni Tower)** constructed — oldest structure in Tamriel | `[CANON — UESP]` |
| ME ~2500–1000 | **Aldmer** (proto-Elves) arrive from Aldmeris and spread across Tamriel; diverge into Altmer, Chimer, Bosmer, Falmer, Dwemer, etc. | `[CANON — UESP]` |
| ME ~2000–1000 | Humans (**Nedes / Atmorans**) begin migrating from Atmora to northern Tamriel | `[CANON — UESP]` |
| ME ~2000 | **Crystal-Like-Law (Crystal Tower)** constructed on Summerset Isle by Altmer | `[CANON — UESP]` |
| ME ~1000–500 | **White-Gold Tower** constructed in Cyrodiil by the Ayleids | `[CANON — UESP]` |
| ME ~1000 | **Night of Tears** — Falmer (Snow Elves) destroy the Atmoran settlement of **Saarthal** | `[CANON — UESP]` |
| ME ~1000 | **Ysgramor** returns from Atmora with the **Five Hundred Companions**; retakes Saarthal, drives Falmer underground | `[CANON — UESP]` |
| ME 1 | **Camoran Dynasty** founded in Valenwood → marks end of Merethic Era and start of First Era | `[CANON — UESP]` |
